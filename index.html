A little more about me ...

Chasing the dream!

I am married with a total of 5 children and live in the Middle Georgia area. I have had a passion for computers since my dad brought home our first Commodore 64. After getting out of the Army, I started my path as a computer programming major. Well, life decided that wasn't going to work and I ended up graduating from Florida State University with a degree in Physical Education. I have been a teacher and a coach for the last 15 years. I felt it was time to pursue my first love and enrolled in the Computer Science program at SNHU. And that's how we got here!

Professional Self-Assessment

My strengths and weaknesses.

Throughout my course work at SNHU, I have had several opportunities to learn the basics of my chosen career path. I have coded in several different languages (C++, Java, Python). I have done virtual labs on setting up routers and servers. I have even done virtual penetration testing. While the course load was comprehensive, I still need more. I feel that my basic coding, hardware set up and troubleshooting, and cyber security skills qualify me for most entry level positions. After graduating, I will be pursuing certifications in networking and security. In this ePortfolio, I have included some examples of my coding and review skills.
